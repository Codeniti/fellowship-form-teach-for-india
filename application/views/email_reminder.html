Essay 1

Please share with us an example of when, for an extended period, you made a substantial commitment to something but at times found it challenging to keep this commitment. Please describe:

  • What you committed to (including the time commitment made, responsibilities held or actions required) and why you made this commitment
  • The challenges you faced to keeping this commitment and when you realised you would face them
  • How you felt about these challenges and what you did about them (please be specific about actions you took, if any)
  • The ultimate outcome and how you felt about this



Essay 2

The Fellowship is a demanding, difficult and rigorous program and we are committed to accepting only applicants who we believe have what it takes to succeed at this challenge. Please explain:

  • What motivated you to apply to TFI and why is it important to you that you are accepted onto the Fellowship?
  • What would success look like for you and your students in the short and long term?
  • What makes you think that you will achieve these goals in the short and long term, and what might hold you and your students back from achieving them?
